### Dress Trousers
Tier: A — Core
Role: Foundation trouser for business, tailoring, formal dressing; clean lines, elegant drape.
Ideal: Rise: Mid/Slightly High · Fit: Tailored/Relaxed Slim, clean seat · Leg: Straight with gentle taper below knee · Hem: No break/slight break · Fabric: 100% wool/tropical wool/high-wool blend · Construction: Flat front, clean waistband, minimal branding · Colours: Charcoal, Dark Grey, Warm Navy · Pattern: Solid, fine texture
Acceptable: Rise: True Mid · Fit: Straight fit · Leg: Classic straight · Hem: Quarter break · Fabric: Wool-poly blend (quality) · Construction: Single pleat (well executed) · Colours: Mid Grey, Chocolate Brown · Pattern: Subtle pinstripe, birdseye
Avoid: Low rise; skinny, baggy, oversized; aggressive taper, wide leg, balloon fit; heavy stacking, full break, cropped lengths; polyester-dominant/shiny/thin synthetic fabrics; cargo pockets, decorative hardware, excessive branding; bright colours, cool light grey, loud patterns; bold checks, high-contrast patterns; heavy break
Colours: Charcoal, Dark Grey, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Rise(20%) > Leg Shape(20%) > Hem & Break(15%) > Fabric(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — sole trouser for tailoring/formal dressing.

### Chinos
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation trouser of the smart-casual wardrobe; bridges casual and refined dressing.
Ideal: Rise: Mid · Fit: Relaxed Slim, comfortable seat/thigh · Leg: Straight with gentle taper · Hem: No break/slight break · Fabric: Medium-weight cotton twill (98–100% cotton) · Construction: Flat front, matte hardware · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Cream, Ecru · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Rise: Slightly High · Fit: Straight fit · Leg: Straight · Hem: Quarter break · Fabric: Cotton with 2% elastane · Construction: Coin pocket · Colours: Stone, Warm Navy, Chocolate Brown · Pattern: Subtle woven texture
Avoid: Low rise; skinny, oversized, baggy; aggressive taper, wide leg, balloon fit; heavy stacking, cropped hems; polyester-rich blends, thin fabrics, shiny finishes; cargo pockets, decorative zippers, elastic cuffs, jogger styling; bright khaki, cool grey, bright blue, pure white; prints; heavy distressing, loud branding
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Cream, Ecru
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Rise(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-value garment category; avoid duplicate role coverage.

### Jeans
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation casual trouser; durability, versatility, visual structure across casual/smart-casual outfits.
Ideal: Rise: Mid · Fit: Relaxed Slim, comfortable seat/thigh · Leg: Straight with gentle taper · Hem: No break/slight break · Fabric: 100% cotton denim or 98–99% cotton denim · Construction: Traditional 5-pocket, minimal branding, matte hardware · Colours: Dark Indigo, Rinse Wash · Details: Clean finish, leather patch, minimal hardware
Acceptable: Rise: Slightly High · Fit: Straight fit · Leg: Straight · Hem: Quarter break · Fabric: Mid-weight stretch denim · Construction: Tonal stitching · Colours: Mid Indigo (clean wash) · Details: Tonal branding
Avoid: Low rise; skinny, spray-on, oversized, baggy; aggressive taper, wide leg, stacked skinny; heavy stacking, cropped hems; polyester-rich blends, lightweight fashion denim, very stretchy denim; decorative embroidery, contrast stitching, large back-pocket designs, excessive branding; heavy fading, whiskering, distressing, acid wash, ripped denim, black denim, very light wash; distressed hems, patchwork, printed graphics, decorative rivets
Colours: Dark Indigo, Rinse Wash
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Wash(20%) > Leg Shape(20%) > Fabric(15%) > Construction(10%) > Rise(5%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-value garment category; dark indigo default.

### Linen Trousers
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Warm-weather trouser prioritizing comfort and breathability.
Ideal: Rise: Mid · Fit: Relaxed Straight · Leg: Straight · Hem: No break · Fabric: 100% linen · Construction: Flat front · Colours: Cream, Ecru, Sand, Olive · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fit: Relaxed Slim · Fabric: Linen-cotton blend · Colours: Tobacco, Stone
Avoid: Low rise; skinny, oversized; polyester-heavy blends; black, bright colours; heavy branding
Colours: Cream, Ecru, Sand, Olive
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Rise(15%) > Colour(15%) > Construction(15%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — summer/resort, drape prioritized over wrinkle resistance.

### Corduroy Trousers
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Cool-weather trouser adding warmth and texture.
Ideal: Rise: Mid · Fit: Relaxed Slim · Leg: Straight with gentle taper · Fabric: Medium-wale cotton corduroy · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green · Pattern: None
Avoid: Very wide wale, thin synthetic corduroy; bright colours
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Colour(20%) > Rise(15%) > Construction(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — autumn/winter texture piece; medium wale balances refinement and texture.

### Shorts
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Hot-weather casual garment; comfort without sacrificing refinement.
Ideal: Rise: Mid · Fit: Relaxed Slim · Length: 5–7 inch inseam, ends 2–5cm above knee · Fabric: Cotton twill or linen · Colours: Olive, Cream, Tobacco, Stone · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Colours: Ecru, Warm Navy
Avoid: Oversized; below-knee or very short athletic cuts; shiny athletic fabric (non-sport); neon, loud prints, camouflage; cargo shorts, distressing, loud branding, oversized pockets, athletic styling outside sport
Colours: Olive, Cream, Tobacco, Stone
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Length(20%) > Rise(20%) > Fabric(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — summer/beach casual, kept clean and understated.
