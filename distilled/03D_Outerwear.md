### Overshirt
Tier: A — Core
Role: Versatile layering piece bridging a shirt and a jacket; adds structure, texture, depth without formality.
Ideal: Layering Silhouette: Room for shirt/polo underneath, clean drape open, hip-length · Fit: Relaxed Slim, clean shoulders, slightly longer than a shirt · Collar: Soft shirt collar, medium proportions · Fabric: Cotton Twill, Cotton Canvas, Herringbone, Brushed Cotton · Construction: Soft structure, matte buttons, minimal branding, straight/rounded hem · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green, Cream, Ecru · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Layering Silhouette: Slightly relaxed · Fit: Tailored · Collar: Camp-inspired (seasonal) · Fabric: Wool blend (cool weather) · Construction: Two chest pockets · Colours: Rust, Warm Navy, Stone · Pattern: Herringbone, subtle texture
Avoid: Shirt-tight fit, oversized fashion fit, long shacket styling; extra slim, boxy, excessively oversized; tiny/oversized/contrast collars; thin poplin, high-shine fabrics; decorative zippers, contrast stitching, heavy branding, excessive utility detailing; bright blue, neon, cool grey, high-contrast colour blocking; large checks, bold plaids, loud prints
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green, Cream, Ecru
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Layering Silhouette(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-ROI layering garment, wear open by default, earth tones dominant.
Tags: outerwear, casual, weekend, layering, all-season, deep-autumn, core, foundation

### Harrington Jacket
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation casual jacket; structure, versatility, refinement across casual and smart-casual settings.
Ideal: Silhouette: Waist-length, structured shoulders, straight body, clean front · Fit: Relaxed Slim, comfortable layering, clean shoulder line · Collar: Classic stand collar, clean rib finish · Fabric: Cotton Twill, Cotton Blend, Suede (premium) · Construction: Raglan sleeves, ribbed cuffs/hem, two welt pockets, quality zipper, minimal branding · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Stone, Warm Navy · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Silhouette: Slightly relaxed body · Fit: Tailored · Collar: Slightly softer collar · Fabric: Waxed cotton · Construction: Tartan lining · Colours: Forest Green, Cream · Pattern: Subtle woven texture
Avoid: Longline cuts, cropped fashion cuts, oversized silhouettes; tight chest, oversized sleeves, excess fabric; oversized/decorative collars; polyester-rich fabrics, shiny synthetics, thin shell materials; decorative patches, contrast zippers, oversized logos, excessive hardware; cool grey, heavy colour blocking; plaids, graphics, bold colour blocking
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Stone, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-ROI jacket category, waist-length improves proportions, earth tones dominant.
Tags: outerwear, smart-casual, weekend, casual-friday, all-season, deep-autumn, foundation-tier, foundation

### Chore Jacket
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Casual layering jacket introducing texture and workwear influence without becoming rugged.
Ideal: Silhouette: Hip-length, straight body, comfortable layering · Fit: Relaxed Slim · Collar: Soft shirt collar · Fabric: Cotton Canvas, Herringbone, Cotton Twill · Construction: Soft structured · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Ecru · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: Heavy cotton · Colours: Forest Green, Stone
Avoid: Cropped cuts; polyester-rich fabric, thin synthetic canvas; bright colours, heavy contrast; oversized fits, large utility pockets, heavy branding, distressing
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Ecru
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — texture over utility styling, clean workwear over rugged workwear.
Tags: outerwear, casual, weekend, all-season, deep-autumn, supporting, accent

### Field Jacket
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Functional casual jacket providing structure, utility, seasonal versatility.
Ideal: Silhouette: Hip-length, defined waist, structured shoulders · Fabric: Cotton Canvas or Waxed Cotton · Construction: Structured, four-pocket layout, hidden placket, matte hardware · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Dark Brown · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: Heavy cotton twill · Colours: Forest Green
Avoid: Oversized military styling, long parka proportions; shiny technical fabrics; velcro; black, camouflage, bright colours; tactical styling, oversized fit, heavy branding
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Dark Brown
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — utility kept understated, silhouette prioritized over pocket count.
Tags: outerwear, casual, weekend, travel, all-season, deep-autumn, specialist, specialist

### Bomber Jacket
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Casual jacket adding structure and visual weight without excessive ruggedness.
Ideal: Silhouette: Waist-length, clean shoulder line, comfortable layering · Collar: Ribbed baseball collar · Fabric: Cotton Twill, Suede, Wool Blend · Construction: Medium structure · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Warm Navy · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: Cotton blend · Colours: Forest Green
Avoid: Cropped fashion fits, oversized bomber styling; shiny nylon, satin; black, high-contrast panels; MA-1 styling with excessive pocket details, bright orange linings, large logos, oversized fits
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — understated over military-inspired, matte finishes age better than shiny.
Tags: outerwear, casual, weekend, all-season, deep-autumn, specialist, specialist

### Suede Jacket
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Elevated smart-casual jacket introducing luxury, texture, refinement.
Ideal: Silhouette: Waist-length, structured shoulders, clean body · Fit: Tailored · Collar: Shirt or Harrington collar · Fabric: Genuine goat or calf suede · Construction: Soft structured · Colours: Tobacco, Chocolate, Dark Olive · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: High-quality suede leather · Colours: Sand
Avoid: Faux suede, plastic-coated finishes; black, bright colours; heavy distressing, decorative hardware, loud branding
Colours: Tobacco, Chocolate, Dark Olive
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(25%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — quality suede worth paying more for, texture is the statement not the design.
Tags: outerwear, smart-casual, dinner, date, all-season, deep-autumn, supporting, foundation

### Overcoat
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Primary cold-weather formal coat, layers over tailoring while maintaining elegant proportions.
Ideal: Silhouette: Knee-length, structured shoulders, clean drape · Fit: Tailored · Lapels: Notch lapel · Fabric: 100% Wool · Construction: Structured · Colours: Chocolate, Charcoal Brown, Warm Navy · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: Wool-cashmere blend · Colours: Camel
Avoid: Very short coats, oversized fashion fits; polyester-heavy fabric; bright colours, busy patterns; trend-driven oversized cuts, large logos, decorative hardware
Colours: Chocolate, Charcoal Brown, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Silhouette(25%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — buy one exceptional overcoat rather than several average ones.
Tags: outerwear, formal, office, tailoring, winter, deep-autumn, specialist, specialist
