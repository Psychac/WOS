# 04 – Wardrobe

## Purpose

This document is the living inventory of the wardrobe.

It records current ownership, identifies strengths and weaknesses, tracks wardrobe gaps, and guides future purchasing decisions.

Unlike the other WOS documents, this document changes regularly.

# Wear Frequency

Each item should be classified as:

* Weekly
* Monthly
* Occasionally
* Rarely
* Never

This identifies garments that provide the highest real value.

---

# Wardrobe Roles

Every owned garment should have a primary role.

Examples:

* Office
* Weekend
* Date
* Travel
* Layering
* Gym

Duplicate roles should be identified.

---

# Gap Analysis

Record genuine wardrobe deficiencies.

Prioritize gaps according to:

* Frequency of use
* Outfit impact
* Versatility
* Opportunity cost

---

# Purchase Roadmap

Current priorities should always be ordered by expected return on investment.

Each purchase should include:

* Priority
* Estimated budget
* Purpose
* Expected outfit increase

---

# Replacement Candidates

Identify garments that should eventually be upgraded.

Reasons may include:

* Poor fit
* Low versatility
* Worn condition
* Better replacement available

---
