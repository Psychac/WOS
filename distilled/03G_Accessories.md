### Leather Belt
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation accessory; provides visual continuity between footwear and clothing.
Ideal: Width: 3–3.5 cm · Leather: Full-Grain Leather · Construction: Single-piece leather, clean painted/burnished edges, straight stitching, minimal reinforcement · Buckle: Brushed silver, rectangular/softly rounded frame, single-prong · Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Tobacco · Details: Minimal branding, tonal stitching, clean keeper loops
Acceptable: Width: 2.8–3.8 cm · Leather: Top-grain leather · Construction: Quality stitched construction · Buckle: Matte gunmetal · Colours: Espresso · Details: Small embossed maker's mark
Avoid: Very narrow fashion belts, wide casual belts; bonded/faux/split leather; laminated construction, thick padded belts, decorative embossing; oversized buckles, polished gold, decorative logos, western buckles; black (first belt); reversible construction, heavy contrast stitching, fashion logos, artificial distressing
Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Tobacco
Evaluation order: Leather Quality(30%) > Construction(25%) > Buckle(20%) > Width(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — one high-quality dark brown belt satisfies most needs; match to footwear family.
Tags: accessories, all-formality, office, weekend, all-season, deep-autumn, foundation-tier, foundation

### Watch
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation accessory; refinement and visual balance across casual and tailored outfits.
Ideal: Case: 36–38mm diameter, thin, balanced lugs, medium bezel · Dial: Clean, applied indices, minimal text, optional simple date · Strap: Dark brown leather or brushed stainless steel bracelet · Movement: Reliable quartz or automatic · Colours: Silver case, White/Cream/Black dial · Details: Minimal branding, sapphire crystal, brushed finishing
Acceptable: Case: 39mm · Dial: Small seconds · Strap: Chocolate or tan leather (casual) · Movement: Solar quartz · Colours: Champagne dial · Details: Mineral crystal
Avoid: Oversized cases (40mm+), thick dive-watch proportions, extremely small fashion watches; skeleton dials, oversized numerals, busy chronographs, bright accent colours; bright coloured straps, silicone (outside sport), large deployant hardware; novelty/fashion-brand movements; decorative bezels, excessive polishing, large logos
Colours: Silver case, White/Cream/Black dial
Evaluation order: Case Proportions(30%) > Dial Design(25%) > Strap/Bracelet(20%) > Movement(15%) > Finishing(10%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — one excellent everyday watch over several fashion watches; brown leather strap first.
Tags: accessories, all-formality, office, weekend, dinner, all-season, deep-autumn, foundation-tier, foundation

### Sunglasses
Tier: A — Core
Role: Primary functional eyewear for outdoor use; complements facial structure without dominating it.
Ideal: Frame Shape: Rectangular or soft square · Material: Acetate or stainless steel · Lens: Polarized, UV400 · Colours: Dark Brown, Tortoiseshell, Matte Black · Details: Minimal
Acceptable: Frame Shape: Wayfarer-inspired · Material: Quality plastic frames · Lens: Standard UV protection · Colours: Olive
Avoid: Oversized frames, tiny round frames, aviators (default recommendation); cheap plastic, fragile hinges; non-polarized lenses, poor UV protection; bright colours, white frames
Colours: Dark Brown, Tortoiseshell, Matte Black
Evaluation order: Frame Shape(30%) > Lens Quality(25%) > Material(25%) > Colour(20%)
Wardrobe role: Core — should complement, not compete with, prescription glasses; timeless shapes over trends.
Tags: accessories, casual, weekend, travel, summer, deep-autumn, core, core

### Wallet
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Daily-carry essential prioritizing function, durability, understated quality.
Ideal: Format: Slim bi-fold · Material: Full-Grain Leather · Construction: Tight stitching, burnished edges · Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate · Details: Minimal
Acceptable: Format: Card holder · Material: Top-grain leather · Colours: Espresso
Avoid: Bulky tri-fold, oversized travel wallets; bonded/faux leather; thick padding, decorative embossing; black (default recommendation), bright colours
Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate
Evaluation order: Format(30%) > Material(25%) > Construction(25%) > Colour(20%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — one quality wallet is sufficient; patina desirable, structural failure is not.
Tags: accessories, all-formality, daily, all-season, deep-autumn, foundation-tier, foundation

### Scarf
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Warmth with understated texture during cooler weather.
Ideal: Material: Wool or Cashmere · Construction: Woven, medium length, soft drape, minimal fringe · Colours: Chocolate, Olive, Tobacco, Charcoal Brown · Pattern: Solid or subtle herringbone
Avoid: Loud tartans, bright colours, heavy logos, bulky knit scarves
Colours: Chocolate, Olive, Tobacco, Charcoal Brown
Evaluation order: Material(30%) > Colour(25%) > Construction(25%) > Pattern(20%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — one versatile wool scarf is sufficient.
Tags: accessories, smart-casual, office, weekend, winter, deep-autumn, supporting, accent

### Cap
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Casual sun protection and travel accessory.
Ideal: Style: Unstructured baseball cap, six-panel, curved brim · Material: Cotton Twill · Colours: Olive, Chocolate, Tobacco, Warm Navy · Details: Minimal branding
Avoid: Snapbacks, flat brims, large embroidered logos, camouflage
Colours: Olive, Chocolate, Tobacco, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Shape(30%) > Material(25%) > Colour(25%) > Details(20%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — own one versatile cap rather than multiple trend-driven options.
Tags: accessories, casual, weekend, travel, summer, deep-autumn, specialist, specialist

### Pocket Square
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Subtle finishing detail for tailored outfits.
Ideal: Material: Linen or Silk, minimal border, soft fold · Colours: Cream, Ecru · Pattern: Solid or minimal border
Avoid: Matching tie sets, loud prints, bright novelty colours
Colours: Cream, Ecru
Evaluation order: Material(30%) = Colour(30%) > Pattern(20%) = Construction(20%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — white or cream linen should be the first pocket square purchased.
Tags: accessories, formal, formal-occasion, dinner, all-season, deep-autumn, specialist, specialist
