### Polo Shirt
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Versatile smart-casual foundation bridging T-shirts and button-down shirts.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, clean shoulders, gentle waist suppression, sleeves to mid-bicep · Collar & Placket: Structured collar, 2-button placket · Fabric: 100% Cotton Piqué/Textured Cotton Knit · Construction: Medium structure, ribbed cuffs, clean hem, minimal branding · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Cream, Ecru, Warm Navy · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Collar & Placket: 3-button placket · Fabric: Cotton blend (minimal synthetic) · Construction: Side vents · Colours: Rust, Terracotta, Chocolate Brown · Pattern: Fine texture, melange knit
Avoid: Extra Slim, oversized, long loose sleeves; soft collapsing/tiny/oversized collars, deep plackets, contrast collars; polyester-rich blends, thin jersey (T-shirt style), high-shine materials; large embroidered logos, contrast trims, decorative piping, heavy branding; bright blue, neon, cool grey, optical white, large colour blocking; high-contrast colour blocking
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Cream, Ecru, Warm Navy
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Collar & Placket(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-value garment, collar quality over brand, piqué default fabric.

### Knit Polo
Tier: A — Core
Role: Elevated smart-casual foundation, more elegant than a standard polo, relaxed and approachable.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, clean shoulders, gentle waist suppression, ribbed hem at hips · Collar & Placket: Soft knit collar, open or clean 3-button placket, medium proportions · Fabric: Fine-Gauge Cotton Knit or Cotton-Linen Knit · Construction: Fully fashioned knit, ribbed cuffs/hem, minimal branding · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green, Cream, Ecru · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Collar & Placket: Structured knit collar · Fabric: Merino wool (cool weather) · Construction: Linked seams · Colours: Rust, Terracotta, Warm Navy · Pattern: Fine knit texture, mélange knit
Avoid: Extra Slim, oversized, long body length; tiny/contrast/oversized collars, deep plackets; polyester-rich blends, thick synthetic knits, loose open knits; large embroidered logos, decorative trims, heavy branding; bright blue, neon, cool grey, colour blocking; graphic patterns, large logos
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Tobacco, Chocolate, Forest Green, Cream, Ecru
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Collar & Placket(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — strongest fit for Modern Creative Minimalist aesthetic, fabric quality/drape over brand.

### Crewneck Sweater
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Versatile layering piece adding warmth, texture, refinement without visual complexity.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, comfortable chest, ribbed hem at hips · Neckline: Classic crewneck, medium rib · Fabric: Merino Wool, Cotton, Cotton-Merino Blend · Construction: Fine to medium gauge knit · Colours: Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Chocolate, Cream, Ecru, Charcoal Brown · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Neckline: Slightly wider crew · Fabric: Lambswool (cool climates) · Colours: Warm Navy, Rust
Avoid: Oversized, skinny, excessively long body; very tight neck, oversized neck opening; acrylic-heavy blends, loose synthetic knits; bright colours, neon, cool grey; large logos, chunky graphics, synthetic-heavy fabric
Colours: Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Chocolate, Cream, Ecru, Charcoal Brown
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Neckline(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — seasonal layering, fabric quality prioritized over thickness, fine-gauge preferred.

### Quarter-Zip Sweater
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Refined layering garment, more structure than crewneck, less formal than tailoring.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim · Neckline: Quarter-zip, fine-gauge knit, quality metal zipper, minimal branding, ribbed cuffs/hem · Fabric: Merino Wool or Cotton · Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Forest Green, Chocolate · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fabric: Cotton-Merino Blend
Avoid: Oversized fit; plastic zippers; contrast panels
Colours: Olive, Tobacco, Forest Green, Chocolate
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Zipper & Neckline(20%) > Fabric(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — autumn/winter layering, wear zipper partially open, keep hardware understated.

### Cardigan
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Refined layering garment providing warmth and versatility without jacket structure.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim · Closure: Button front, fine to medium gauge knit, minimal branding, ribbed cuffs/hem · Fabric: Merino Wool or Cotton · Colours: Olive, Chocolate, Forest Green, Charcoal Brown · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Closure: Shawl collar (casual use)
Avoid: Chunky oversized knits; decorative patterns
Colours: Olive, Chocolate, Forest Green, Charcoal Brown
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Construction(20%) > Colour(15%) > Closure(10%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — classic button-front over fashion-forward, fine-gauge offers greatest versatility.
