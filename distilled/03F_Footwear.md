### Minimal Leather Sneakers
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation casual shoe; greatest versatility across casual and smart-casual outfits with refined appearance.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Low-profile silhouette, rounded/soft almond toe, clean proportions, moderate toe box · Upper: Full-Grain Leather, smooth matte finish · Construction: Cup sole, clean stitching, minimal panel construction, quality lining · Sole: Low-profile rubber, white/off-white sidewall, minimal tread · Colours: Off-White, Cream · Details: Minimal branding, tonal stitching, plain laces, clean heel
Acceptable: Last & Shape: Slightly elongated toe · Upper: Top-grain leather · Construction: Well-executed cemented construction · Sole: Warm gum sole · Colours: White, Taupe · Details: Small embossed logo
Avoid: Bulky athletic silhouettes, square toes, aggressively pointed toes, oversized platform soles; faux/corrected/patent leather, heavy synthetic panels; excessive overlays, visible air units, complex technical designs; thick platform soles, aggressive lug soles, contrast-coloured midsoles; black, bright colours, neon, colour blocking; running shoe silhouettes, chunky "dad" sneakers, large logos
Colours: Off-White, Cream
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(5%) > Details(5%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-ROI purchase, off-white/cream preferred over optical white, keep design clean.

### Penny Loafers
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Foundation smart-casual shoe; bridges casual and tailored dressing with elegance.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Rounded/soft almond toe, moderate forefoot volume, low-profile silhouette · Vamp & Apron: Medium vamp, clean hand-sewn apron, balanced penny strap · Upper: Full-Grain Leather or Suede · Construction: Blake Stitch or Goodyear Welt · Sole: Leather or thin rubber · Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Tobacco · Details: Penny strap, minimal branding, tonal stitching
Acceptable: Last & Shape: Slightly elongated toe · Vamp & Apron: Slightly higher vamp · Upper: Nubuck · Construction: High-quality cemented construction · Sole: Combination sole · Colours: Dark Olive Suede, Espresso · Details: Hand-sewn apron
Avoid: Narrow Italian lasts, square toes, aggressively pointed toes, chunky fashion loafers; very high vamp, bulky apron stitching, decorative tassels, horsebit hardware; faux/patent/corrected leather; heavy synthetic construction, visible glue, excessive decorative stitching; thick lug soles, chunky platform soles; black (first pair), bright colours, two-tone finishes; heavy branding, oversized silhouettes
Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Tobacco
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Vamp & Apron(15%) > Sole(5%) > Colour & Details(5%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-ROI footwear purchase, last shape over brand, brown is first choice.

### Derby Shoes
Tier: A — Core
Role: Primary business and formal shoe; more versatile than an Oxford while remaining refined.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Soft almond toe, moderate forefoot volume, balanced proportions · Lacing: Open derby construction · Upper: Full-Grain Leather · Construction: Goodyear Welt or Blake Stitch · Sole: Leather or combination sole · Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Espresso · Details: Minimal
Acceptable: Last & Shape: Rounded toe · Upper: High-quality calf leather · Construction: High-quality cemented · Colours: Warm Burgundy
Avoid: Square toes, pointed fashion toes, very narrow lasts; closed Oxford lacing, decorative lacing; faux/patent leather; heavy synthetic construction; thick lug soles, chunky rubber soles; black (first pair); heavy broguing, large logos
Colours: Dark Brown, Chocolate, Espresso
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(5%) > Lacing(5%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — first formal shoe should be brown Derby, not black Oxford; elegant proportions over decoration.

### Chelsea Boots
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Primary boot of the wardrobe; adds seasonal versatility while staying refined smart-casual.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Rounded soft almond toe, medium forefoot volume, slim ankle profile · Upper: Suede or Full-Grain Leather · Construction: Blake Stitch or Goodyear Welt · Sole: Leather or thin rubber · Colours: Dark Brown Suede, Chocolate, Tobacco · Details: Minimal
Acceptable: Last & Shape: Slightly elongated toe · Upper: Nubuck · Construction: High-quality cemented · Colours: Espresso
Avoid: Square toes, aggressive points, bulky work-boot lasts; faux/patent leather; heavy synthetic construction; lug soles, thick commando soles; black (first pair), bright colours; Cuban heels, decorative buckles, heavy branding
Colours: Dark Brown Suede, Chocolate, Tobacco
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — suede offers greater versatility than smooth black leather; keep silhouette sleek not rugged.

### Oxford Shoes
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Most formal shoe in the wardrobe; reserved for business formal dress, ceremonies, traditional tailoring.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Soft almond toe · Lacing: Closed Oxford · Upper: Full-Grain Leather · Construction: Goodyear Welt · Sole: Leather · Colours: Dark Brown, Espresso · Details: Plain cap toe, minimal detailing
Avoid: Heavy broguing, square toes, thick rubber soles, bright colours
Colours: Dark Brown, Espresso
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Specialist — purchase after Derby shoes unless formal dress becomes frequent.

### Chukka Boots
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Relaxed smart-casual boot bridging sneakers and Chelsea boots.
Ideal: Last & Shape: Rounded soft almond · Upper: Suede · Construction: Blake or Goodyear Welt · Sole: Crepe or thin rubber · Colours: Tobacco, Chocolate, Dark Brown · Details: Two or three eyelets, clean construction
Avoid: Thick work soles, bulky lasts, decorative hardware
Colours: Tobacco, Chocolate, Dark Brown
Evaluation order: Last & Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — suede gives best balance of texture and versatility; keep simple not rugged.

### Sandals
Tier: C — Specialist
Role: Warm-weather casual footwear prioritizing comfort while maintaining refinement.
Ideal: Style: Leather slide or minimal sandal, minimal straps, understated design · Upper: Leather · Sole: Thin rubber · Colours: Dark Brown, Tobacco · Details: Minimal
Avoid: Athletic sandals, neon colours, thick foam soles, large logos
Colours: Dark Brown, Tobacco
Evaluation order: Shape(30%) > Construction(25%) > Upper Material(20%) > Sole(15%) > Colour(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — one quality leather pair is sufficient.
