### Formal Shirt
Tier: A — Core
Role: Refined foundation for tailoring, business wear, formal occasions.
Ideal: Fit: Tailored/Relaxed Slim · Collar: Semi-spread/Moderate spread · Fabric: Cotton Poplin/Twill · Construction: Medium structure, single barrel cuffs, clean placket · Colours: White, Off-white, Light Blue · Pattern: Solid, Fine Stripe
Acceptable: Fit: Slightly relaxed classic · Collar: Classic point · Fabric: Pinpoint Oxford (less formal) · Construction: French cuffs (occasion-dependent) · Colours: Pale Cream · Pattern: Small conservative check
Avoid: Extra Slim/oversized/boxy cuts; extreme cutaway/oversized/button-down collars; polyester blends, linen, satin, high-shine fabrics; soft collapsing collars, decorative trims, excessive branding; loud colours, busy patterns; large checks, bold stripes, high-contrast prints; transparent fabric; collar collapse
Colours: White, Off-white, Light Blue
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(20%) > Collar(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-formality shirt, pairs with tailoring only.

### Oxford Cloth Button-Down (OCBD)
Tier: S — Foundation
Role: Most versatile shirt in the wardrobe; bridges formal and casual dressing.
Ideal: Fit: Tailored/Relaxed Slim · Collar: Soft button-down, natural roll · Fabric: Medium-weight 100% cotton Oxford cloth · Construction: Medium-soft structure, rounded hem · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Cream, Ecru, Light Blue · Pattern: Solid, University Stripe
Acceptable: Fit: Straight classic · Collar: Slightly structured button-down · Fabric: Heavy Oxford cloth · Construction: Locker loop, box pleat · Colours: White, Tobacco, Muted Beige · Pattern: Fine Bengal Stripe, small gingham
Avoid: Extra Slim/oversized/boxy; fused collars, tiny collars, long exaggerated points; stretch Oxford, polyester blends, thin poplin mislabeled OCBD; decorative trims, contrast stitching, excessive logos; bright blue, cool grey, neon; large plaids, bold prints, high-contrast patterns; collar without natural roll; extremely thin Oxford cloth
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Cream, Ecru, Light Blue
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Collar(15%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — highest-value shirt category, primary versatility driver.

### Casual Button-Down
Tier: A — Core
Role: Everyday shirt for relaxed smart-casual dressing; adds warmth, texture, personality.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, straight to gently tapered · Collar: Soft Spread/Soft Point, one-piece · Fabric: Cotton Twill, Brushed Cotton, Herringbone, Dobby · Construction: Soft, unstructured, matte buttons · Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Rust, Terracotta, Cream, Ecru · Pattern: Solid, fine texture, herringbone
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Collar: Camp collar (summer only) · Fabric: Soft Oxford cloth, cotton-linen blend (summer) · Construction: Single chest pocket · Colours: Warm Navy, Muted Beige, Chocolate Brown · Pattern: Small checks, windowpane, muted plaid
Avoid: Extra Slim/oversized/boxy/skinny; stiff fused collars, extreme cutaway, very small collars; polyester blends, high-shine fabrics, very thin fabrics; contrast trims, decorative stitching, epaulettes, heavy branding; bright blue, cool grey, neon, optical white; large checks, loud florals, high-contrast/busy geometric patterns; trend-driven designs
Colours: Olive, Dark Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Rust, Terracotta, Cream, Ecru
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(20%) > Collar(20%) > Construction(15%) > Colour(15%)
Wardrobe role: Foundation — everyday relaxed smart-casual driver, earth tones dominant.

### Linen Shirt
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Warm-weather shirt prioritizing breathability, comfort, effortless elegance.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, full rollable sleeve · Collar: Soft Spread/Soft Point · Fabric: 100% linen, heavyweight where available · Construction: Soft, matte buttons, rounded hem · Colours: Cream, Ecru, Olive, Sage, Sand, Tobacco · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Collar: Band collar (summer only) · Fabric: Linen-cotton blend (≥55% linen) · Construction: Single chest pocket · Colours: Rust, Terracotta, Muted Beige, Warm White · Pattern: Very subtle woven texture
Avoid: Extra Slim, oversized, cropped lengths; stiff fused collars, button-down collars, extreme cutaways; polyester blends, thin transparent linen, high-stretch fabrics; contrast trims, decorative stitching, heavy branding; bright blue, neon, cool grey, black (most warm-weather use); large checks, loud florals, high-contrast prints; skinny fit, overly structured construction
Colours: Cream, Ecru, Olive, Sage, Sand, Tobacco
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Colour(20%) > Construction(15%) > Collar(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — warm-weather/resort use, natural wrinkling embraced not avoided.

### Flannel Shirt
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Cool-weather shirt providing warmth, texture, seasonal depth with refined casual appearance.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, clean shoulders · Collar: Soft Spread/Button-Down · Fabric: 100% cotton brushed flannel, medium weight · Construction: Soft, matte buttons, rounded hem · Colours: Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Chocolate Brown, Rust, Cream · Pattern: Solid, muted plaid, windowpane
Acceptable: Fit: Tailored · Collar: Soft Point · Fabric: Heavyweight flannel, cotton-wool blend · Construction: Single chest pocket · Colours: Burgundy, Charcoal Brown, Warm Navy · Pattern: Large tonal check
Avoid: Extra Slim/oversized/boxy/skinny; stiff fused collars, extreme cutaways; polyester blends, thin "fashion flannel," high-stretch fabrics; decorative stitching, contrast trims, heavy branding; bright red, bright blue, cool grey, neon; buffalo check in bright colours, loud tartans, high-contrast patterns; decorative patches; thin flannel marketed as premium
Colours: Olive, Forest Green, Tobacco, Chocolate Brown, Rust, Cream
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Colour(20%) > Construction(15%) > Pattern(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — autumn/winter layering, should read refined not rugged.

### Chambray Shirt
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Lightweight casual shirt with denim's visual character but greater versatility/comfort.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, clean shoulders · Collar: Soft Spread/Button-Down · Fabric: 100% cotton chambray, medium weight · Construction: Soft, lightweight · Colours: Mid Blue, Light Blue · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Colours: Faded Indigo
Avoid: Extra Slim, oversized; denim marketed as chambray, polyester blends; very dark indigo, black, fashion washes; heavy contrast stitching, distressing
Colours: Mid Blue, Light Blue
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Colour(20%) > Construction(15%) > Details(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — relaxed smart-casual, lighter alternative to denim.

### Denim Shirt
Tier: B — Supporting
Role: Rugged casual shirt providing visual weight and texture; use sparingly.
Ideal: Fit: Relaxed Slim, comfortable layering fit · Collar: Soft Spread · Fabric: Mid-weight 100% cotton denim · Construction: Soft structured · Colours: Mid Indigo, Dark Indigo · Pattern: Solid
Acceptable: Colours: Rinse wash
Avoid: Skinny, oversized; stretch denim, heavy washes, distressing; acid wash, light distressed blue, black denim; heavy fading, contrast stitching, western yokes, snap buttons
Colours: Mid Indigo, Dark Indigo
Evaluation order: Fit(30%) > Fabric(25%) > Colour(20%) > Construction(15%) > Details(10%)
Wardrobe role: Accent — one denim garment per outfit, styled clean to avoid workwear overload.
