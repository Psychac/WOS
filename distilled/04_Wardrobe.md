## Inventory Categories
Tops: Shirts, Polos, T-Shirts, Knitwear, Overshirts, Jackets
Bottoms: Dress Trousers, Casual Trousers, Jeans, Shorts
Footwear: Sneakers, Loafers, Boots, Sandals
Accessories: Watches, Belts, Bags, Sunglasses, Fragrance

## Per-Item Fields
Wear Frequency: Weekly / Monthly / Occasionally / Rarely / Never
Wardrobe Role: Office / Weekend / Date / Travel / Layering / Gym (one primary role; flag duplicates)

## Gap Analysis
Prioritize by: frequency of use > outfit impact > versatility > opportunity cost.

## Purchase Roadmap Entry
Priority · Estimated budget · Purpose · Expected outfit increase. Order by expected ROI.

## Replacement Candidate Triggers
Poor fit · low versatility · worn condition · better replacement available.

## Purchase History Entry
Date · cost · brand · initial reason · post-purchase review. Use to inform future decisions.
