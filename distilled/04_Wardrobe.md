## Bottlenecks
- Single everyday shoe (Salvador Sneakers) — every formal and casual outfit routes through it; most severe bottleneck.
- Cream Chino/Jeans Hybrid — 6 tops depend on this one trouser.
- Black Formal Trousers — 3 of 6 formal shirts depend on this one trouser.
- No knitwear layer — no option between shirt/tee and overshirt for transitional weather.
- Formal-only dress trousers — Friday casual dressing fully absorbed by the two hybrids, compounding their concentration risk.
